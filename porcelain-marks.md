<section id="hutschenreuther" class="marks-section">
<h2>Hutschenreuther</h2>
<p>German porcelain manufacturer founded in Hohenberg, Bavaria in 1814.</p>

<div class="marks-subsection">
<h3 class="mark-ruler">Abt. Paul Müller</h3>
<p class="mark-explanation">Hutschenreuther acquired the <strong>Paul Müller</strong> porcelain factory in 1917.</p>

<div class="mark-entry">
    <div class="mark-images">
        <span class="img-zoom"><img src="/assets/images/hutschenreuther-abt-paul-muller-serving-platter-mark.webp" alt="Hutschenreuther Abt. Paul Müller mark" style="transform: scale(1.3);"></span>

</div>
</div>

<div class="marks-subsection">
<h3 class="mark-ruler">L.H.S.</h3>
<p class="mark-explanation">L&hairsp;.&hairsp;H&hairsp;.&hairsp;S.&hairsp; stands for "&hairsp;Lorenz Hutschenreuther Selb&hairsp;". <strong>Lorenz Hutschenreuther</strong> took over his father's porcelain business in 1855 and established the <strong>Selb</strong> factory in 1857.</p>

<section id="rosenthal" class="marks-section">
<h2>Rosenthal</h2>
<p>German porcelain manufacturer founded in Selb, Bavaria in 1879.</p>

<h3 class="mark-ruler">SELB-BAVARIA</h3>
<p class="mark-explanation">After 1933, Rosenthal marked its products "&hairsp;Germany&hairsp;" instead of "&hairsp;<strong>Bavaria</strong>&hairsp;" in a changing political&nbsp;climate.</p>
